How it works

From onboarding to distribution, the workflow is the product.

Whether you arrive as an investor or an issuer, the same primitives carry you from intent to settled position.

  1. 01

    Verify once.

    Complete a single KYC and accreditation flow that satisfies every deal, every jurisdiction. Identity, wallet, and entity records live with you, not the originator.

  2. 02

    Browse curated deals.

    Filter by tenor, yield, asset class, jurisdiction, or rating. Open the full memo, run scenarios, and add the position to a watchlist.

  3. 03

    Subscribe and settle.

    Allocate digital cash, sign documentation, and receive a tokenised position in one signed transaction. No wires, no paper, no waiting.

  4. 04

    Track, report, exit.

    Receive distributions, reporting, and tax statements automatically. Trade out on the secondary market or hold to maturity. Either way the data follows the asset.

Ecosystem

Built with the partners that keep capital markets honest.

Demox integrates with regulated custodians, fund administrators, trustees, auditors, and identity providers. Our standards are designed so each role can be operated by any qualified institution.

Custody

Qualified custodians safeguarding investor wallets, deal escrow, and waterfall accounts under segregated mandates.

Fund administration

Independent administrators producing NAVs, investor statements, and distribution waterfalls on a continuous basis.

Audit + assurance

Big-four-aligned audit firms providing financial assertions and proof-of-reserve attestations on every issuance.

Identity + compliance

Accredited identity providers operating perpetual KYC, sanctions screening, and accreditation checks.

Trustees

Independent trustees representing investor interests and enforcing covenants across every issuance.

Legal counsel

Specialist securities and structured finance counsel reviewing every deal template and amendment.
